Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Facebook-connected application (“the App”).

1. Information We Collect

When you connect the App with Facebook, we may collect:

  • Basic profile information you grant access to (such as name, email address, profile ID).
  • Any additional data that you explicitly approve via Facebook Login permissions.

We do not collect information from your Facebook account without your permission.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the App’s features.
  • Authenticate your account and personalise your experience.
  • Maintain and improve the App’s performance and reliability.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • With service providers who help operate and host the App under confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. Data is retained only for as long as necessary to operate the App or as required by law.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can control the data we have access to by managing the App’s permissions in your Facebook settings. You can:

  • Remove the App from your Facebook account at any time.
  • Revoke specific permissions granted to the App.

When you remove the App, we will delete associated data that is no longer required for legal or operational purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users below the minimum age permitted by Facebook in your region. We do not knowingly collect data from children in violation of Facebook’s policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The “Last updated” date reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated Policy.
